Introduction

There are five parts included in our project- a timeline of arts’ general types, a tree of arts’ types and objects, a word cloud of their key words, a list for masterpieces, and also, a gallery to present the arts to users.
The timeline: We design a timeline to describe the relationship between the centuries when the arts were created and the types of them. On this time line, we can click on it to look for more details of the periods we selected, zoom in and out to see it more clearly , and select the periods to get more information in the parts of tree, word cloud and masterpieces.
The tree: The tree is designed to make our users get more information about the types and objects of the arts in the period they selected from the timeline.
The word cloud: From the word cloud parts, users will get detailed description of the arts from the periods they selected, and when they click on the words, the webpage of the word will appear to tell more details about them.
The list: Here we present a list to make users know the most famous artists of the period they selected.
The gallery: In this gallery, uses can see the arts directly from their desired periods.
